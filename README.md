# AI4S-YB

> Open Source, Open Science, Open Future

AI4S-YB is a place for open work around AI for Science.

We share code, tools, datasets, and research in public. We care about clarity, reproducibility, and work that others can build on.

## What You Can Find Here
- Repositories for ongoing projects
- Tools and code released for public use
- Datasets, notes, and supporting materials
- Work in progress that reflects how we think and build

## Why We Work in the Open
- Open source makes tools easier to use, test, and improve
- Open science makes research easier to examine, repeat, and extend
- Open work creates more room for collaboration across people and fields

## Looking Ahead
We do not see openness as an extra step after the work is done.

We hope to keep building small, useful, reliable pieces that help research move more freely. If enough work is shared this way, the future of AI for Science can be more open, more grounded, and more collaborative.
